FAQs

What kind of wedding is it?

It's a sit-down lunch, so please come between 12.00pm - 12.20pm! Lunch will be served at 12.40pm right after the bride and groom march in.

When is the RSVP date?

31st October is the latest RSVP date. Please RSVP via our online RSVP form or WhatsApp Sara or Farhan.

What if I have a dietry restriction? Do you serve vegetarian/vegan options?

Please let us know and we'll do our best to accomodate. However we apologize if we're unable to meet your requirements!

We currently have a limited amount of non-beef tables, please let us know if you'd like to be placed on them.

We have a vegan/vegetarian dish (dahl) but we cannot guarantee if it's pure vegan/vegetarian.

What is my table number?

We will let you know one week before the event what is your table number. Alternatively, you can find out from our usherers on the day of the event and they'll help you out.

Can I bring a +1? How about kids?

Yes, you absolutely can bring a +1! We can also allow you to bring your kids if it means we can have you attend our special day, just let us know in advance so we can allocate the seats accordingly.

Is there any dress code? Colour theme?

The bride's family will be wearing blue and the groom's family colours is gold. If you're neither, you're welcomed to wear anything as long as it's smart casual :)

When is the nikah? (ROM)

9.30am on the same day (1 December), same hall and venue. Only family and some close friends will be attending.

When is the tea ceremony?

10.30am on the same day (1 December), same hall and venue. Family members who want to be a part of this special occasion should be present by 10.15am.

About the Venue

a. How do we get to Dewan Merak Kayangan?

You need to drive, unfortunately. There is no public transport that I know of.

b. Are there parking spaces?

Yes, there is free parking available on site.

c. Is Dewan Merak Kayangan wheelchair accessible? What if I have mobility issues?

Please let us know if you are wheelchair bound or have mobility issues so we can allocate you to seating that is on ground level. The surrounding areas is wheelchair accessible, but the hall itself is split-level.

Do you have a hotel for guests? Where do you recommend I stay?

If you are family from outside of KL, please refer to the family wedding party. If you are a regular guest, we currently don't have hotel options for you. But if you need some recommendations, please reach out to us and we can try to give you some advice.

Can I take photos and post on social media?

Yes you can! Our official hashtag is #FarhanSara2019. However, please be respectful and mindful when taking photographs. Please do not block or disturb the official photographer / videographer.

Who do I contact if I have any more questions?

You may contact Sara or Farhan.
