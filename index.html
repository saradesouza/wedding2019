Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of wedding is it?

It's a sit-down lunch, so please come on time between 11.45pm - 12.10pm as to not disrupt the ceremony.

When is the RSVP date?

14th of November is the latest RSVP date. Please RSVP via our online RSVP form or WhatsApp Sara or Farhan.

What if I have a dietry restriction?

All the food served is Halal. We will be serving these dishes during the wedding reception, dome-style, so you can pick and choose based on your dietry requirement:

  • Vegetarian Dahl
  • Sweet & Sour Fish
  • Beef Rendang
  • Honey Chicken
  • Sotong Sambal (Spicy Squid)
  • Papadom
  • Nasi Minyak (Ghee Rice)

Because the wedding party will be busy with the event itself, we cannot guarantee that there will not be any cross-contamination during the preparation of the dishes.

What is my table number?

We will personally inform you of your table number one week before the event. Alternatively, you can find out your table number at the reception table on that day itself.

Can I bring a +1 that you didn't specifically invite?

We can allow for +1s if there are extra seats, just let us know in advance.

Is there any dress code? Colour theme?

The bride's family will be wearing blue and the groom's family colours is gold. If you're neither, you're welcomed to wear anything that's smart casual :)

When is the Akad Nikah? (Registration Of Marriage)

9.30am on the same day (1 December), same hall and venue. Only close family and a few close friends will be attending.

When is the Tea Ceremony?

10.30am on the same day (1 December), same hall and venue. Family members who want to be a part of this special occasion should be present by 10.15am.

About the Venue

a. How do we get to Dewan Merak Kayangan?

Drive: Use this link to navigate using Google Maps or Waze.

Public Transport: Take the Kelana Jaya LRT line to Damai LRT station and take a Grab/taxi to the location, or walk 15 minutes to the venue.

b. Are there parking spaces?

Yes, there is free parking available on site.

c. Is Dewan Merak Kayangan wheelchair accessible? What if I have mobility issues?

Please let us know if you are wheelchair bound or have mobility issues so we can allocate you to seating that is on ground level. The surrounding areas is wheelchair accessible, but the hall itself is split-level.

Do you have a hotel for guests? Where do you recommend I stay?

If you are family from outside of KL, please refer to the family wedding party. If you are a regular guest, we currently don't have hotel options for you. But if you need some recommendations, please reach out to us and we can try to give you some advice.

Can I take photos and post on social media?

Yes you can! Our official hashtag is #FarhanSara2019. However, please be mindful and respectful when taking photographs. Please do not block or disturb the official photographer / videographer.

Who do I contact if I have any more questions?

You may contact Sara or Farhan directly :)
